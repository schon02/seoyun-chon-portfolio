Hello! My name is Seoyun and I recently graduated from Tufts University with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and a minor in Engineering Management. Here, you can learn a little bit about me and see some of the work I've done so far. Thank you for visiting!

Fun fact: I love cars! Here is a picture of me with Adam Lz at Formula Drift 2024. All of the pictures on this website (except this one) have been taken by me!

I was raised in the Seattle area and grew up participating in educational programs sponsored by local tech companies. My favorites programs were DigiGirlz, sponsored by Microsoft, and the Girls Who Code Summer Immersion Program, sponsored by AT&T. When it came time to decide on a college major, the decision came easily for me, based on how much I enjoyed thse programs. Reflecting on my college experience, some of my most treasured moments involved collaborating and problem-solving with my peers on coding assignments. I hope to continue on with this dynamic in my professional career, and take on roles where I am able to work with people of many different backgrounds and experiences.
